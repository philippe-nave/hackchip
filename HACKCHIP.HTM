All right, I admit it.

I had way too much time on my hands, and I played a lot of Chip's Challenge. Then I got frustrated, as hackers often do, and I hacked the game. I dumped the data file out in hex, then pored over it until patterns revealed themselves... then, I wrote a program to dump out the passwords for all the levels.

The encryption scheme was simple, and here are the results!
